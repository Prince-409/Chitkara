Piano Instrument

The piano is a timeless instrument known for its elegance, versatility, and expressive beauty. It plays a vital role in both classical and modern music.

The piano is a keyboard instrument that produces sound by striking strings with hammers. It is used in solo performances, orchestras, and contemporary bands. Our college music club provides high-quality pianos for practice and performance alike.

Learning the piano enhances finger coordination, musical theory understanding, and creativity. Students explore genres ranging from classical symphonies to modern pop and jazz compositions.
