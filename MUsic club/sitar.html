The sitar is a classical string instrument played by plucking its strings to produce rich, resonant tones. It holds a special place in Indian classical music and is also embraced in fusion and world music genres. Our college music club provides authentic sitars and skilled guidance for students passionate about this graceful art form.

Learning the sitar develops patience, precision, and emotional depth. From meditative ragas to contemporary compositions, students experience a journey of rhythm, tradition, and musical devotion.
